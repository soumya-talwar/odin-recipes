Fruit salad

Description

A refreshing colorful assortment of cut fruits.

Ingredients

  • Watermelon
  • Muskmelon
  • Papaya
  • Strawberry
  • Kiwi

Steps

  1. Slice watermelon in half, then quarters. Dice a quarter of a watermelon into bite sized pieces.
  2. Do the same with a muskmelon. Use half a muskmelon.
  3. Use half a papaya, remove the seeds.
  4. Wash strawberries, remove the stem, cut in half or quarters.
  5. Peel the kiwi, slice into discs.
